Behind the Magic of Auto-Instrumentation (Grafana ❤️🔥 OpenTelemetry Community Call #6)

2026-Feb-27
opentelemetry , english , video , grafana labs , grafana otel community call , auto-instrumentation , instrumentation

In this community call with my coworker Liudmila Molkova, we pulled back the curtain on OpenTelemetry’s auto-instrumentation capabilities. We explored how auto-instrumentation works under the hood, when to use it versus manual instrumentation, and the trade-offs involved. The discussion covered language-specific implementations, the Collector’s role in auto-instrumentation, and practical tips for getting started with zero-code observability.
