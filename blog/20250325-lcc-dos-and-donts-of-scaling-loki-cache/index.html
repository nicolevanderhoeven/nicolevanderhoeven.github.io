Do's and Don'ts of Scaling Loki Cache (Loki Community Call March 2025)

2025-Mar-25
loki , caching , scaling , loki community call , video , english , grafana labs

In this Loki community call, Poyzan (Staff Engineer on the Loki team) returns this time with Paul Rogers (Staff Engineer on the Loki team) to talk all about Loki cache! This topic was heavily discussed in our “How to run Loki at scale on Kubernetes” call. Some of the topics we will cover:

  • Types of cache and cache tiering
  • Best practices for scaling your cache
  • Understanding failure scenarios with Loki cache and how to detect them
