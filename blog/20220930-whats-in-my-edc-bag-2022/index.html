What's in my EDC Bag (2022)

2022-Sep-30
video , english , gear

What’s in my tech EDC bag? In this video, I show you the sling bag I bring around on a daily basis, and every bit of gear in it. This is my everyday carry for 2022, and everything I take with me to stay productive on the go.

Here’s everything I mentioned in the video above that I could find a link for:

  • Samsung Galaxy Active 2: my smartwatch
  • d20 spinner ring: a ring that acts like a 20-sided die for roleplaying games on the go
  • Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 3: my primary mobile phone
  • Aer Day Sling 3 X-Pac: my sling bag of choice
  • Nitecore Tini 2 USB C flashlight: tiny USB-C rechargeable flashlight
  • Nite-Ize locking S-biners: for a little extra security for zippers
  • Orbitkey holder: to hold my keys
  • Nano bag 2.0: compactable bag
  • Kable Card: all essential cables and adapters, a light, a mobile stand, and a memory card reader all in one card
  • Aurtech bluetooth keyboard: portable bluetooth keyboard
  • Samsung Galaxy S Pen Fold Edition: stylus for my mobile
  • Sony WF 1000 XM3: wireless noise-cancelling earbuds
  • Spigen Arc Powerstation 20W: small GaN charger
  • Secrid mini-wallet: my minimalist wallet
  • Dispel Dice Lilac Song: a full-sized d20 I carry around
  • Sprawlopolis: small cooperative card game
  • LIE: fun party game for 2-6 players and is the size of a pack of gum
  • Victorinox Jetsetter: small, travel-friendly multitool
  • Mighty Hanks: microfiber on one side, cotton on the other
  • Burts Bees: lip balm
  • Small red Sharpie: mini marker
  • Joby Gorillapod GripTight Smartphone Micro Tripod: tripod for my mobile
  • Samsung Galaxy Note 10+: secondary mobile
  • Topo designs micro pouch: small pouch
  • Tile Mate Pro: bluetooth tracker
  • Trezor Model T: hardware wallet and security key
