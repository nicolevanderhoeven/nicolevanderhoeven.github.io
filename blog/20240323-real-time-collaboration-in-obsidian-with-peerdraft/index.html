Real-time Collaboration in Obsidian with Peerdraft

2024-Mar-23
obsidian , peerdraft , english , video

Working with others in Obsidian hasn’t always been the easiest task. Obsidian Peerdraft is a new community plugin and accompanying web app that brings real-time collaboration to Obsidian. In this video, I tell you how to use Peerdraft, collaborate with a friend live while using it, and interview the developer of the app and plugin, Dominik Schlund.
