All About Structured Metadata in Loki (Loki Community Call August 2025)

2025-Aug-12
loki , loki community call , observability , grafana , english , video

In this video, Jay Clifford and I talk to Senior Software Engineer Salva Corts Sánchez from the Loki team about structured metadata in Loki. We discuss what it is, why you might want to use it, and also how to query it. Structured metadata is especially useful if you’re instrumenting your system with OpenTelemetry, so we also touch on that.
