DevOps Culture from a Tester's Perspective (Adobo & Avocados #24)

2023-Dec-15
adobo & avocados , video , english , testing , devops , agile , testing

We’ve all heard of DevOps, but what is DevOps from a tester’s perspective? How can we embrace this culture? In this episode of Adobo and Avocados, the one and only Lisa Crispin talks to my friend Marie Cruz and me about DevOps culture from a testing mindset, what holistic testing is really about, and her journey to being one of the well-respected voices in the testing community.
