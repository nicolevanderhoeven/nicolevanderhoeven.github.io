Evaluating LLM Agents in the Real World: Inside Grafana Assistant

2026-Jan-25
opentelemetry , english , video , grafana labs , grafana otel community call , genai , llm , ai , grafana assistant

This video offers a behind-the-scenes look at how we evaluate LLM agents in production at Grafana Labs. Using Grafana Assistant as a case study, I discuss the challenges of evaluating non-deterministic AI systems, the metrics that matter for agent quality, and practical approaches to monitoring and improving AI agent performance over time. The talk covers evaluation frameworks, feedback loops, and lessons learned from running AI agents at scale.
