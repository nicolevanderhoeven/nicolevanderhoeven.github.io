Hi, I'm Nicole van der Hoeven. I'm a Performance Testing Advocate at k6.io. I've spent my career making software faster as a load tester, and I'm passionate about helping oithers do the same. I firmly believe that quality software can change the world. I'm a proud polynational (Filipino, Australian, and Dutch), polyglot, and polymath. I write technical and fictional articles and books, film and produce videos, create podcasts, speak at conferences, and otherwise just generally enjoy getting to know people.
