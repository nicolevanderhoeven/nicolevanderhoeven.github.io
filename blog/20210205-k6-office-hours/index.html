The first ever k6 Office Hours

2021-Feb-05
video , english , k6.io , performance

The k6 virtual office is open!

The first time I heard about office hours was from Notion. Recently, Obsidian went in on this trend as well. The concept involves opening your virtual doors regularly to your community, to answer questions and share best practices.

A big part of being a Developer Advocate is talking to people, and even conferences these days are mostly pre-recorded. I kind of miss being able to interact with people. I miss the immediacy of live. So I talked Simme Aronsson into doing a k6 Office Hours with me.

We’re both new to livestreaming, so there’s a lot that we’ve learned and will be improving in future videos, but here’s our first attempt!
