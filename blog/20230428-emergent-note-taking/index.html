Emergent Note Taking

2023-Apr-28
english , video , obsidian , pkm , learning in public

Emergent note taking is the process of turning individual notes into something more than the sum of their parts and combining them in different ways. In this video, I talk about how to take notes to be found and how to explore your vault in ways to help ideas emerge in Obsidian.
