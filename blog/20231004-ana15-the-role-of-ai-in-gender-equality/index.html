The Role of AI in Gender Equality with Nicola Martin (Adobo & Avocados #15)

2023-Oct-04
adobo & avocados , video , english , diversity , testing , ai , gender

In this episode of Adobo and Avocados, my friend Marie Cruz and I chat with Nicola Martin, Head of Quality Engineering at Adarga, to talk about the role of AI, technology, and education in gender equality.

Nicola is a Council and Committee Member for the British Computer Society and WomenTech Global Network. She is also a mentor, speaker, and panelist speaking on subjects such as AI and quality, software testing, and diversity in tech.
